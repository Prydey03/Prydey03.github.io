Resources

Here are some valuable resources to help you learn more about responsible drinking, safety, and related topics:

  • Alcohol and Its Effects on Health

    A comprehensive resource by Healthdirect Australia explaining how alcohol impacts physical and mental health.

    Learn More

  • Understanding Blood Alcohol Concentration (BAC)

    Provided by the Alcohol and Drug Foundation, this resource offers insights into BAC levels and their implications.

    Learn More

  • Alcohol and Drug Information Service (ADIS) - New South Wales

    A confidential helpline offering support, information, and resources for those dealing with alcohol and drug-related issues in New South Wales.

    Learn More

  • DirectLine - Victoria

    A helpline providing counseling and support services for individuals and families affected by alcohol and drug use in Victoria.

    Learn More

  • DrinkWise Australia

    A non-profit organization dedicated to promoting a healthier drinking culture and reducing alcohol-related harm in Australia.

    Learn More

Seeking Help?

If you or someone you know is struggling with alcohol-related issues, consider reaching out to the following organizations in Australia:

    Alcoholics Anonymous Australia offers support and a fellowship of individuals who share their experiences and help each other overcome alcohol addiction.

Stay informed about alcohol-related issues and responsible drinking through the following Australian resources and organizations:
